I.How Do We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

To ensure the product and services can operate as intended, we need to process your personal information in certain circumstances. We have implemented the appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of personal information in accordance with the requirements of the law.

1.Purpose of collecting personal information

Your personal information will be collected and used for the following purposes during your use of this product:

(a) to help you become a user of UPET and keep your personal information secure;

(b) to help you control the machine and manage your cat's activities through UPET;

(c) to make it easier for you to quickly and conveniently resolve any issues you have while using the product;

2.Types of personal information to be collected

(1) Personal information you provide to us directly
■ Identification information, we collect your mobile phone number or email address to help you manage your smart cat box for security. If you refuse to provide your identification and  related credentials information, you will be restricted to some of UPET's functions or services. You can choose to enter your name, gender, date of birth, and other information to improve your account,you have no any restrict if not provide this information. After you voluntarily deregister your account, your personal information will be deleted or anonymized as required by law.
■ Location information and Bluetooth access. After you grant us permission to access geolocation (under the Google framework of the Android system, the location information must be obtained before a Bluetooth connection is set up) and Bluetooth, the app will collect your geolocation information and connect to the litter box through the Bluetooth scan. The geolocation information will not be stored and will not be uploaded to the cloud. The legality of such information processing is based on your consent. You will be unable to pair the device with your mobile phone if you do not provide the above personal information.

■ Network connection information. Wi-fi data(SSID, Wi-Fi password) will be connected and stored locally only on the device and will not be uploaded to the cloud, to help your smart cat box connected to internet. The legality of such information processing is based on your consent. If you refuse to provide the above personal information, you will not be able to use the network connection function.

(2) Information required to be collected to improve the product and provide the services

■ Services and after-sales related personal information. If you contact our customer service hotline to report any issues, we may ask you to provide the necessary personal information for identity verification in order to protect the security of your account. We may also store your contact information (mobile phone number, email address, or other contact information you use or voluntarily provide), the record and content of your call, and other information necessary to provide and record the customer services for you.You can also use the feedback function to report problems to us. We need to collect device information such as your device SN, fault description, contact information provided by you, and uploaded device logs. 
■ Cat profile: when you use UPET to sync the data of the relevant device, we will record your cat's profile, including the avatar, nickname, breed, gender, date of birth, activities, toileting, pictures and weight of the cat. Such information is stored separately from your identity information, is not identifiable or associated with it, and is generally not considered personal information.
■ Cat Video Information: We may collect video information related to cat activities using the camera of the Smart Cat Litter Box, such as movement in the area, cat toileting, and triggering of smart scenes so that you can view and manage your cat's relevant toileting data through UPET. The video data includes the recording date, time, triggering event, video length, and video content. Such information is stored separately from your identity information, is not identifiable or associated with it, and is generally not considered personal information.Only the videos authorized by the User will be subject to data processing.

■ Real-Time Video：Allows users to view the feed from the cat litter box camera in real-time via the App. The video stream is live data and will not be stored. To protect your privacy, please ensure the account bound to your cat litter box is not used by others. The camera, from certain angles, may capture faces. However, the device does not have facial recognition capabilities, and any captured facial information will not be processed. You can control the activation or deactivation of the real-time video function by sliding the camera cover on the device or by exiting the "Video Feed" mode within the App.

■ Cat Photos：Certain models of smart litter boxes support the "Cat Recognition" feature. To use this feature, you need to upload at least one set of cat photos in advance for registering your cat's facial recognition features. You can upload or delete these cat photos within the "Cat Data" section of the App.
■ Device and App Operation Information: On the basis of our compliance with our legal obligations, we will receive and record your Smart Cat Litter Box's configuration and cell phone model information, including device failure information, configuration information, and performance information, to analyze the product for operation and maintenance and security assurance, in accordance with the specific permission granted by you during product installation and use. Relevant information includes cell phone model, system version, system time zone, system language, network operator, App version number, device SN, android_id, regular cleaning time, time requiring no disturbance, and other information. Such information cannot identify or be associated with a natural person. For other device information, we will ask you whether you grant the corresponding permission, and we will not receive your relevant personal information until you agree.

II.How Do We Use Cookies and Similar Technologies
We may use analytics cookies to collect information about how visitors use our website. The information these cookies collect includes how often you use the app, events that occur within the app, cumulative usage, performance data, and app crashes. We do not link the information stored in the analytics software with any personal information you submit in the mobile application.

To achieve some functionality, we delegate to processors to process data for the purpose we require.. To protect data security, we strictly monitor the use of security of SDKs used by service providers to obtain information.

(2) the information is provided in accordance with laws and regulations, legal process, or mandatory administrative or judicial requirements.
(3) the information is provided as agreed in the relevant agreements signed by you (including the online electronic agreements and the corresponding platform rules) or other legal documents; and
(4) we will require the new company or organization that will hold your personal information to continue to be bound by the Policy in the event of a merger, acquisition, asset transfer, or similar transaction involving the transfer of personal information, or we will require that company or organization to seek your authorization again.

We process and back up personal information through our global operation and control facilities. Refusing automated decision-making: you have the right to refuse the processing of your personal information based solely on automated decision-making if the automated decision-making has legal effect on you or otherwise has a significant impact on you. We will not use your personal information to participate in automated decision-making that has legal or other significant effects.

Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent to the privacy policy through 'About - Privacy Policy' in the app. Once you withdraw consent, you will log out of the APP and your account, and we will no longer process your personal information.
